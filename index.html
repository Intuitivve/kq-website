How KQ tuition works

Short, focused sessions

Most sessions are 60 minutes. Enough time to learn, practise and reflect – not enough time to switch off.

Tiny groups, clear targets

Max 5 learners per group. Everyone knows exactly what they’re working on this week, not just “maths”.

Data and gut instinct

We track marks and patterns, but we also actually talk to the kids. Confidence, habits and mindset count too.

Why parents pick KQ over big-box tuition

  • Sessions run by someone who genuinely likes maths, logic and teaching – not a franchise script.
  • Local to Royal Wharf, so KS2 parents don’t spend half the evening commuting.
  • Real exam-style questions from early on – not endless colouring worksheets pretending to be learning.
  • Clear communication with parents. If something isn’t working, you’ll hear it directly, not via a termly report.
  • Flexible mix of in-centre and online, depending on what actually suits the child.
