KS1 (Years 1–2)

Gentle, confidence-first tuition for early readers and number explorers.

We keep things light, playful and structured so KS1 learners actually want to come back next week.

Book a KS1 chat

Who this KS1 programme is for

  • Children in Year 1 or Year 2 who are a bit wobbly with phonics or basic number facts.
  • Kids who understand more than they can show on paper, and need a confidence boost.
  • Young learners who are flying ahead and need something more interesting than “add 3 apples”.

What we work on in KS1

Reading & phonics

  • Segmenting and blending
  • High-frequency word recognition
  • Reading short texts out loud with expression

Writing & spelling

  • Sentence structure and punctuation basics
  • Neat handwriting habits
  • Spelling patterns, not just random lists

Early maths

  • Number bonds to 10 and 20
  • Add/subtract with pictures and mental methods
  • Simple word problems kids can actually explain

Session rhythm

  • Warm-up games (5–10 mins) – Quick phonics or number games to switch the brain on, not off.
  • Focused teaching (30 mins) – We pick one or two key skills and practise them in small steps.
  • Confidence finish (10–15 mins) – Learners show what they’ve nailed – on mini whiteboards, in a short read-aloud, or a quick quiz.

    • What KS1 parents can expect

    • Short, direct feedback after sessions – one thing that went well, one thing to watch.
    • Simple at-home practice ideas that don’t require an arts degree or a laminator.
    • A focus on attitude and enjoyment, not just ticking off levels.
