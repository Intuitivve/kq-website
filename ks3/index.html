Who this is for

  • Year 7–9 students who did “okay” at primary but feel lost with secondary maths or English.
  • Learners who are coasting and need a push to aim for higher sets.
  • Students who want GCSE grades 7–9 and understand that foundation work starts early.

KS3 Maths focus

  • Number fluency and fractions clean-up
  • Algebra basics: expressions, equations, inequalities
  • Shape, area, perimeter and angle reasoning
  • Introduction to graphs and coordinates

KS3 English focus

  • Reading unseen texts and actually understanding subtext
  • Paragraph structure and argument building
  • Analytical writing for prose and poetry
  • Technical accuracy (spelling, punctuation, sentence control)

Study habits that survive GCSE

  • How to plan revision without a meltdown
  • Using checklists and spacing practice
  • Turning teacher feedback into concrete next steps
